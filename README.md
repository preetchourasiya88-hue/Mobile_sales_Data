📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard Project – Power BI

I built a Mobile Sales Dashboard in Power BI to analyze mobile sales performance and transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights.
The dashboard helps in tracking sales trends, customer behavior, brand performance, and revenue growth through interactive visualizations and KPI metrics.

The main goal of this project was to:

Monitor overall sales performance
Identify top-selling mobile brands and models
Analyze customer purchasing trends
Compare sales across different regions and time periods
Support data-driven decision-making
📊 KPI Cards Used in the Dashboard

I used multiple KPI cards to measure overall business performance:

✅ Total Sales

Shows the complete revenue generated from mobile sales.

✅ Total Quantity Sold

Represents the number of mobile units sold.

✅ Total Profit

Displays overall profit earned from sales.

✅ Average Sales Value

Shows the average revenue per transaction/order.

✅ Top Selling Brand

Highlights the highest-performing mobile brand.

✅ Customer Count

Tracks the number of customers making purchases.

✅ Monthly Growth %

Measures sales growth month-over-month.
